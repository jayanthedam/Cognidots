  • API connection setup (usually takes less than a day)
  • Initial data sync to train our AI models
  • Front-end widget installation (simple code snippet)
  • Testing and configuration

Most clients are fully integrated within 2-3 days with minimal technical resources required from your team.

  • Training Sessions: For your team to maximize platform benefits

Our Enterprise plans include additional support features like priority response times and direct developer access.
